About

Brussels sprouts. Dislike them or hate them, they're as much a part of Christmas as Morecambe & Wise, turkey, or children crashing their new bikes into your hedge.

So for the BMB Christmas Game, we want you to fire the little green blobs into the gaping mouths of our hungry Christmas Characters - and if you're in the top 3 at the end of the day, we'll send a fantastic prize your way.

Playtime is 10am-2pm, 12-14 Dec, so get it in your diaries and get involved.
